Biography

I’m a political scientist at the University of Oxford. I pursue a DPhil in the interdisciplinary Centre for Socio-Legal Studies, working on rule of law backsliding in Central Eastern Europe and European responses to protect democracy, rule of law, and judicial independence in Member States. In my research, I use mixed methods. I trace political processes, using both written sources and semi-structured elite and expert interviews and complement this with insights from computational methods to collect and analyse digital texts.
